PRAYAS

PRAYAS is an outreach program by IEEE SJCE WIE that provides in
equitable education outcomes to the underprivileged children.
The volunteers visited one Orphanage on a weekly basis to interact
with the students there and help them out in their academic activities.



ABOUT PRAYAS

▪An effort by our team to provide under privileged a positive learning environment that will help them unleash their potential.
▪ This includes frequent visits to Divya Deepa and Vijaya Educational Institution.
